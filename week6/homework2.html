After the finale of Geats, King-Ohger will be joined by Kamen Rider Gotchard in the Super Hero Time block.

After advancing to the final round, Michinaga joined Ace Ukiyo and Takeshi Goutokuji as one of the three remaining competitors. Alongside Shirowe, he transforms into Buffa and is deployed the city. After witnessing Goutokuji save them, Michinaga accused him of saving Keiwa Sakurai and Neon Kurama to score extra points. When Goutokuji was instantly killed by the Slug Fortress Jyamato, Michinaga laughed since it meant he had one less obstacle to overcome. To Michinaga's disapproval, Ace showed up and defeated the last boss, ending Michinaga's chances of victory.In the new world, Michinaga lost all of his memories. As the next edition of the DGP began, Michinaga accepted Tsumuri's invitation to participate in the new tournament, restoring his memories as Kamen Rider Buffa in the process. He would also meet with Keiwa and Neon again, though initially doubting their odds of survival. During the Treasure Hunting mission of the competition, Michinaga would acquire the Zombie Raise Buckle again, but is forced to relinquish it after losing a bet with Ace during the Zombie Hunting mission. He also forms a temporary truce with Kanato Sumida in exchange for the Magnum Buckle, before Kanato himself was eliminated from the tournament.

When Michinaga Azuma was in high school, he and his best friend Toru Imai fought a group of bullies before they were taken in by a "boss". At some point later in their adulthood, they became construction workers. However, Toru became a contestant of the Desire Grand Prix where he was killed by two enemy Kamen Riders. After Toru died in Michinaga's arms, Michinaga was saved by Ace Ukiyo, who rescued him from two Jyamato. Michinaga was later knocked unconscious by Desire Grand Prix staff and taken away..
